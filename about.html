ABOUT THE COMPANY

GSD Painting and Decorating are one of London‘s premier commercial painting and decorating contractors. We are covering the entire city of London and the South East. With years of expertise, we deliver high quality work on every project we undertake. We are working on Hotels, Bars, Clubs, restaurants, High End Residentials, Leisure Centres, school, stadium, office fits out and lot lots more.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

GSD Painting and Decorating philosophy is to deliver the highest quality commercial painting and decorating contractors in London. Every customer, quote, project is an opportunity for us to prove our ability delivering great top notch projects.

We care about our customers and we are committed to understand them. Don’t hesitate to contact us for further information , we would be happy to put our expertise in commercial painting and decorating at your disposal.

You can have a look at our portfolio to see the different commercial painting and decorating projects we have been working on for the past few years.

All of our painters and decorators have many years of experience, initially training under official four-years apprenticeship schemes, gaining City and Guilds qualifications in decorating – the professional way. We have all learnt the trade the old fashioned way, and take great pride in our work. These qualities are instilled in every member of the team.

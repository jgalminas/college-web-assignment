ABOUT US

OVER 10 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE
IN PAINTING AND DECORATING

As Painters and decorators in London, GSD Painting and Decorating has quickly emerged as one of London’s premier commercial painting and decorating companies. Our experienced and dedicated staff provide an excellent service, whether your project is residential, commercial or requires a specialist finish.

GSD Painting and Decorating undertakes both internal and external painting and decorating projects. These range from hotels, high end residential homes, leisure decorating, school buildings, restaurants, clubs, bars, stadium renovation, office outlets and many more. The services we offer are available through out the City of London as well as the South East.
